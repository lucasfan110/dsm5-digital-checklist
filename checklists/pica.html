Pica

  1. A. Persistent eating of nonnutritive, nonfood substances over a period of at least 1 month.
  2. B. The eating of nonnutritive, nonfood substances is inappropriate to the developmental level of the individual.
  3. C. The eating behavior is not part of a culturally supported or socially normative practice.
  4. D. If the eating behavior occurs in the context of another mental disorder (e.g., intellectual developmental disorder [intellectual disability], autism spectrum disorder, schizophrenia) or medical condition (including pregnancy), it is sufficiently severe to warrant additional clinical attention. 372
  5. Coding note:

    The ICD-10-CM codes for pica are F98.3 in children and F50.89 in adults.

  6. Specify if:

    In remission: After full criteria for pica were previously met, the criteria have not been met for a sustained period of time.
